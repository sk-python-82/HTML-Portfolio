Sina's Recipe For Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 eg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread floor

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablesppon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. mix the yeast with milk,sugar,eggs
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. add in the floor and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead th dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover it with plastic wrap.leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hrs
  6. After the dough has doubled in size roll it out into a large rectangle
  7. Melt the butter for the filling and mix it in the sugar and cinnamon.
